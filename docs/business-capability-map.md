# Business Capability Map

## Commerce

### Customer Management

Manage customer identities, accounts, relationships, and business interactions.

### Product Management

Maintain product information, categorization, and availability.

### Pricing Management

Define and maintain pricing structures and commercial rules.

### Order Management

Manage the complete order lifecycle from creation through fulfillment.

---

## Operations

### Inventory Management

Maintain inventory visibility, accuracy, and stock control.

### Fulfillment Management

Coordinate picking, packing, shipping, and delivery workflows.

### Procurement Management

Manage purchasing activities and supplier sourcing processes.

### Supplier Management

Maintain supplier relationships, performance, and operational collaboration.

---

## Administration

### Identity & Access Management

Manage users, roles, permissions, and authentication.

### Configuration Management

Control business rules, system settings, and platform behavior.

### Audit & Compliance

Track operational activity and maintain accountability.

---

## Analytics

### Commerce Analytics

Measure commercial performance and customer activity.

### Operational Analytics

Measure operational efficiency and execution performance.

### Decision Support

Provide reporting, insights, and KPI visibility for stakeholders.
